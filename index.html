F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is the expected turnaround time?

    We always push to provide you our analysis as quickly as possible, with most products available within 2 business days.

  • What payment methods are accepted?

    We accept all major credit cards and will also permit payment through invoice on net 15-day terms.

  • Can I track my research?

    Yes, if you email us we can let you know the status and expected turnaround time

  • Can you integrate my existing research?

    Yes, if you send us any current research or analysis that you have, we can include these in addition to our research pulled from our databases.

  • Can you expedite my research?

    Yes for custom projects we can expedite an order to overnight or two-day turnaround for an additional fee.

  • Can I cancel my research?

    Currently we only support research and analysis in English but we are constantly updating our offering and expect to offer Spanish and French products by Q3 2020.

  • What languages do you support?

    Currently we only support research and analysis in English but we are constantly updating our offering and expect to offer Spanish and French products by Q3 2020.

  • Can I change my research?

    Yes, if you let us know within 2 hours of your order. After two hours have elapsed, we will ask that you submit another order to ensure that you get the analysis you need.

  • Can I get a subscription?

    Yes, we are planning to roll-out a subscription plan for lower research prices by Q3 2020.
